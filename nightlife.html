Berghain & Panorama Bar
The Church of Techno

Berghain is one of the worldwide most famous clubs. It’s the church of Techno with the best sound system you can get. So be prepared to dance for hours and listen to top quality music. Inside you'll find two different clubs: Berghain and Panorama Bar. The club is open no stop every week-end from Friday 'til Monday morning ! . A “lifetime experience” is definitely the expression that better describes the time you’ll have here.

The motto of the club is "Do what you want but stay in communication".

The KitKatClub attracts people from all over Europe and the world because of its music selection (techno and trance music) and its sexually uninhibited atmosphere. Salon by Wild Renate
Labyrinth of circus fun

It’s name already describes the kind of atmosphere you’ll find: WILD. Their main attraction is a huge and real labyrinth. So if you get lost, you’ll be condemned to live inside a club for the rest of your life. Not bad, huh? Their parties are original and also really diverse. Depending on the night, you will listen to the most different and bizarre kinds of music and meet people you’ve never expected before.

Monkey Bar - rooftop
One of the most celebrated light festivals worldwide - Semptember 3-12

On the 10th and top floor you find the Monkey Bar rooftop bar, offering unbeatable panoramic views over the city and the Berlin Zoo. The name actually comes from the views you get over the zoo's monkey house. Trendy, vibrant and cosy-chic in setting, the rooftop bar offers a wide range of creative and crafted signature cocktails, along with some wine, beers and various bar bites. This place is usually buzzing and there are different live DJs playing every night.

A-Trane jazz club
On lazy Sundays in Berlin this is the perfect place to hang around and sing your heart out

At the A-Trane jazz club you can relax to the sounds of the saxophone, discover the latest musical trends, enjoy the wee small hours or simply swing along. Despite its small size, with only a hundred seats, it’s a big venue on Berlin’s jazz scene.
