Berghain & Panorama Bar
The Church of Techno

Berghain is the most well-known club in Berlin - and also one of the worldwide most famous clubs. It’s the church of Techno with the best sound system you can get. So be prepared to dance for hours and listen to top quality music. Inside you'll find two different clubs: Berghain and Panorama Bar. If you’re the kind of person who appreciates good techno music, you’ll feel like you’re in paradise once you step inside Berghain. Their line up is always impeccable and you’ll probably listen to the best DJ's blasting their best tunes out loud, every weekend, Friday 'til Monday morning ! . Inside Panorama Bar, you’ll find a more cheerful atmosphere, where they usually play all variations of house music. It’s the perfect place to have a drink, calm down and then go back to Berghain. Berghain became a meeting point for the clubbers of Berlin and it’s already part of the city culture! A “lifetime experience” is definitely the expression that better describes the time you’ll have here.

The KitKat Club
The motto of the club is "Do what you want but stay in communication".

The KitKatClub is said to attract patrons from all over Europe and other parts of the world because of its music selection (techno and trance music) and its sexually uninhibited . A strict dress code needs to be respected in order to get into the KitKatClub door, often enforced by Kirsten herself, and requiring fetish, latex, leather, kinky, high style, and glamour. Through the years the KitKatClub has become a notorious Berlin institution.

Salon by Wild Renate
Labyrinth of circus fun

If you’ve ever dreamed about clubbing in a circus, you’ll fall in love with this club. It’s name already describes the kind of atmosphere you’ll find: WILD. Their main attraction is a huge and real labyrinth. So if you get lost, you’ll be condemned to live inside a club for the rest of your life. Not bad, huh? Their parties are original and also really diverse. Depending on the night, you will listen to the most different and bizarre kinds of music and meet people you’ve never expected before. It’s not one of the most famous clubs in town but it definitely has its charm.

Monkey Bar - rooftop
One of the most celebrated light festivals worldwide - Semptember 3-12

On the 10th and top floor of the amazing 25hours Bikini Hotel you find the Monkey Bar rooftop bar, offering unbeatable panoramic views over the city and the Berlin Zoo. The name actually comes from the views you get over the zoo's monkey house. Trendy, vibrant and cosy-chic in setting, the rooftop bar offers a wide range of creative and crafted signature cocktails, along with some wine, beers and various bar bites from the adjecent NENI restaurant.With seating both inside and out on the rooftop terrace, this place is usually buzzing and there are different live DJs playing every night. As this is a very popular place and a bit of a line can be expected. But well worth it, as this is one of the very best rooftop bars Berlin has to offer.

A-Trane jazz club
On lazy Sundays in Berlin this is the perfect place to hang around and sing your heart out

At the A-Trane jazz club you can relax to the sounds of the saxophone, discover the latest musical trends, enjoy the wee small hours or simply swing along. Despite its small size, with only a hundred seats, it’s a big venue on Berlin’s jazz scene.

Citadel Music Festival
The Citadel Music Festival stages every summer a series of rock, pop and electronic music concerts at an amazing location

The Spandau Citadel is one of the best preserved renaissance stronghold, which generate a very specific atmosphere, attracting international artists as well as music fans from all over the world. The artists belong to bands from the punk section, alternative, jazz or even the German pop music section. Among them have been legends such as Bob Dylan, The Beach Boys and Björk.
