Open air cinema
Movies under the spangled sky

Cinema under starry skies is something Berlin delivers well during balmy summer nights. Open-Air Cinemas (called Freiluftkinos in German) are spread across the German capital in parks, old warehouses, and historic courtyards. Grab some popcorn and choose from romantic classics, thrillers, cult films, foreign language pickings, or art house cinema.

Festival of lights
One of the most celebrated light festivals in the world

For 10 nights, 100 different locations between the Brandenburg Gate and the TV Tower will be lit up at night, displaying visually-stunning light installations on historical monuments, buildings, squares and streets spanning a total of 100.000 square meters. it can easily be explored by foot, bike, bus, boat or limousine - making it accessible to all.
